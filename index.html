About Us

Impande TechDev Solutions is a business that brings innovative solutions in the IT industry and provides business solutions through the evolution of the digitalized world and artificial intelligence. We also empower the youth by guiding them to improve their IT skills needed in the business world and the communities at large.

Mission

  • Become the fastest growing entity that leads IT services, including software applications.
  • Innovate the digital space through ideas that bring solutions to all industries.
  • Empower young IT enthusiasts with skills and guidance to impact lives and businesses.

Vision

  • Provide inventive solutions at reasonable costs.
  • Deliver partnerships through strategic advice and solutions.
  • Offer high-tech hardware and software solutions to clients.
  • Empower youth from as young as 7 years to graduates to impact the business world.
